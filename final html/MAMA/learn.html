Sounds of the cosmos + robot ping pong mayhem

With a full complement of new exhibitions, MAMA is humming with visitors enjoying the subtle (and not so subtle) sounds of our Autumn artists.

Material Sound invites visitors to experience art with all of their senses. Generate sounds with the currents in your body, conduct a chaotic orchestra with a robot ping pong machine, and relax to the soothing tones of the moon's glow through a solar powered amplifer.

Portraits of powerful local women are featured in Nat Ord's Rise! and intricate prints by nine artists from Cicada Press come together in Ngurrambaa.
